# Provenance regarding Islam
Primary sources include *The Quran*, *Sahih Al-Bukhari* (Hadith), and Al-Ghazali's *The Incoherence of the Philosophers* (for Occasionalism). Islam emphasizes Submission (*Islam*) to the Will of Allah and the perfection of the Sacred Law (*Sharia*).

## Reality is Real, Knowable, and Consequential (Friction: Occasionalism)
"Allah created the heavens and the earth in truth." (Quran 39:5).
*Al-Ghazali on Causality:* "The connection between what is habitually believed to be a cause and what is habitually believed to be an effect is not necessary... The combustion of cotton, for example, at the time of its contact with fire, may be admitted [as occurring], but that it is done *by* the fire is rejected." (*The Incoherence of the Philosophers*, Discussion 17).
"There is no agent except Allah." (Ash'arite maxim).
"Allah is the Creator of all things." (Quran 39:62).
*(Note: Occasionalism denies secondary causation, creating friction with scientific realism).*

## Humans are Limited and Selfish (Friction: Fitra)
"So direct your face toward the religion, inclining to truth. [Adhere to] the fitrah of Allah upon which He has created [all] people." (Quran 30:30).
"Every child is born on the Fitra [natural disposition/purity]; it is his parents who make him a Jew or a Christian." (Hadith, Bukhari).
"Indeed, mankind was created anxious: When evil touches him, impatient, And when good touches him, withholding." (Quran 70:19-21).
*(Note: While human weakness is acknowledged, the doctrine of innate purity (Fitra) contrasts with Original Sin/Constrained Vision).*

## Tiered Self-Anchored Responsibility (Incompatible: Tribalism)
"The believers are but brothers." (Quran 49:10).
"A Muslim is a brother of another Muslim." (Hadith, Bukhari).
"O you who have believed, do not take the Jews and the Christians as allies. They are [in fact] allies of one another." (Quran 5:51).
"Let not the believers take the disbelievers as allies rather than the believers." (Quran 3:28).
*(Note: Responsibility is bifurcated: one set of duties for the Ummah, another for the Kafir).*

## Goodness is to Live and Live Well (Friction: Submission)
"It is not for a believing man or a believing woman, when Allah and His Messenger have decided a matter, that they should [thereafter] have any choice about their affair." (Quran 33:36).
"And whoever desires other than Islam as religion - never will it be accepted from him." (Quran 3:85).
"Fight them until there is no [more] fitnah [disbelief/dissent] and [until] the religion, all of it, is for Allah." (Quran 8:39).
*(Note: The 'Good' is defined as the establishment of Islam, not necessarily universal flourishing).*

## Procedural Symmetry (Incompatible: Sharia)
"Fight against those who do not believe in Allah... until they give the jizyah willingly while they are subdued." (Quran 9:29).
"A Muslim is not killed for [killing] an infidel." (Hadith, Bukhari 6915).
"Men are in charge of women by [right of] what Allah has given one over the other." (Quran 4:34).
*(Note: Sharia explicitly encodes asymmetry based on religion and gender).*

## Humility (The Criticism Test)
"Whoever changes his religion, kill him." (Hadith, Bukhari 6922).
"If anyone insults the Prophet... he must be killed." (Consensus of Schools).
"They wish you would disbelieve as they disbelieved so you would be alike. So do not take from among them allies." (Quran 4:89).
*(Note: Historical and textual penalties for apostasy and blasphemy negate the possibility of open critique).*
