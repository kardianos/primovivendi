## Additional Points

[L1] Social Outliers [idea:P010]

  [L2] Society will always have outliers. Never normalize outliers but society must judge if an outlier is good or bad.

  [L2] Most outliers will be bad, but societies must judge if the outlier is good or bad.

  [L2] If outliers build and create surplus, or create beauty, and have children, perhaps they are good.

  [L2] If they take, destroy, fail to have good children, then they are bad.

  [L2] Suppression of outliers by default is protective and not punitive.

  [L2] If someone is an outlier, hold them to higher standards, not lower standards.

  [L2] Outliers are not obligated to conform, but society is not obligated to carry your costs either.

  [L2] The guiding principle: Civilization advances by suppressing parasitic behavior and insuring surplus-producing behavior. [idea:P011]

  [L2] People are free to vary. Behaviors are not free to impose costs.

  [L2] When societies confuse tolerance with virtue, or deviation with value, they either stagnate or dissolve. [tie:D009]
