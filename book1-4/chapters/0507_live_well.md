## Live Well.

The principles are now in your hands. Begin with yourself. Begin now.

Reality is real. Seek what corresponds to it.

Humans are limited. Design your life around what you can actually do.

Goodness is surviving and thriving. Choose what sustains and improves the conditions for life.

Priority is tiered and self-anchored. Secure the core before extending outward.

Justice is procedural symmetry, scoped to tiers. Apply the same rules to yourself that you would apply to others in the same position.

Live the bundle. The alternative is already visible in the results it produces. The choice is yours.
