## Methods of Debate that Reference Truth

The principles provide a consistent method for evaluating ideas in real time. The method requires running every claim through the full set rather than allowing any single principle to stand alone.

### How to Run an Idea Through All Five Principles

When someone advances a claim, ask whether it assumes reality is real. Ask whether it accounts for human limits. Ask whether it leads to surviving and thriving. Ask whether it respects tiered priority. Ask whether it applies procedures symmetrically within the correct scope.

If the claim fails any one of these tests, it fails the bundle. If someone argues from one principle while ignoring the others, they are cherry-picking. The bundle does not support cherry-picking.

### How to Recognize When Someone Is Operating on Inverted Premises

Listen for the signals. Reality is described as constructed or as narrative. Human limits are treated as obstacles to be overcome rather than constraints to be designed around. Survival is treated as morally suspect. Tiered loyalty is pathologized as bigotry. Procedural fairness is dismissed in favor of outcome equality.

A further signal is the selective force of logic. A person advances an argument with clear logical structure only while it produces the preferred conclusion. The same standard of consistency is discarded the moment the conclusion turns inconvenient. This is not practicing logic as a procedure that binds both sides. It is using the form of logical argument as a weapon while reserving the right to exempt oneself from its discipline. This is the breach of procedural symmetry at the level of reasoning. Outcome or power is the actual rule. Logic is retained only when it serves that rule.

These are not random disagreements. They are the inverted bundle showing itself. Name it.

### How to Refuse the Compassion Weapon

When someone invokes your care for children, the weak, or the disadvantaged to demand that you betray an inner tier for an outer one, recognize the pattern. The response is direct. My compassion for my children is precisely why I refuse. Refuse without apology.

### How to Distinguish Discovered from Constructed in Live Argument

When an opponent calls something constructed, ask the clarifying question. Is it constructed in form, or constructed in substance? Force the distinction into the open. Discovered things resist decree. Constructed forms can be redesigned. The argument is usually about which category the thing falls into, not about whether the categories exist.

### How to Use the Mirror Productively

Do not engage the inverted bundle principle by principle. Engage it as a bundle. Ask the interlocking question. If you soften on equity, what happens to your constructivism? If you soften on constructivism, what happens to your equity? Show that their principles also interlock and that softening one leaks reality back in. This is more honest and more effective than line-item refutation.

### How to Scope Your Arguments

Arguments about the family tier should stay at the family tier. Arguments about the citizen tier should stay at the citizen tier. Leaking one tier's logic into another is the most common source of confused debate. When someone says but what about the children in other countries, they are leaking an outer tier's logic into an inner tier's domain. Bring the scope back.

### How to Test Your Own Reasoning

Apply the matrix to your own positions. If your preferred policy fails the reality check, or demands unlimited sacrifice, or treats an outer tier as more important than an inner one, your policy is failing the bundle. Adjust or abandon it. The bundle is not a weapon you wield only against others. It is a discipline you apply to yourself first.
