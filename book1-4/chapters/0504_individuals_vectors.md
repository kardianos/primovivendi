## Individuals as Vectors of Society

A society receives its direction from the individuals who compose it. When those individuals point the same way, the society gains a coherent trajectory and a shared purpose that can be sustained across time.

Orienting toward truth, surviving, and thriving is the bare minimum baseline. Below this threshold, no society can compound its capabilities or transmit its gains to the next generation.

Objecting to orientation toward truth, or objecting to the concept of truth itself, is sociopathic regardless of the justification offered. A society that treats truth as optional severs itself from the only reliable mechanism for correcting error and for aligning action with reality.
