## Orient to a Core Positive

The frame is not a list of grievances. It is a positive vision about how to live, how to build, how to raise children, and how to improve the conditions that make flourishing possible.

Where the inverted bundle has gained ground, the measurable results are exactly what the five principles predict: decline, parasitism, demographic surrender, and erosion. Reciting the list of failures is not the project. The project is to build the alternative. A society that orients toward the principles can compound. A society that does not will continue to record the same predictable losses until it changes course or ceases to exist.
