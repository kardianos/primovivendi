*   **The Incompatibility:** This creates an epistemic wall. If the doctrine allows for lying to "prudent" ends, then any "moderate" claim of peace is untestable. You cannot distinguish between a genuine reformer and a tactical deceiver.

The current "Moderate" strategy of citing peaceful Meccan verses while ignoring violent Medinan ones is not just an error—it is an exercise of Taqiyya. By presenting the "expired" version of Islam as the "true" version, the apologist deceptions the outsider.

*   **The Bait: Meccan Context (Weakness):**

    *   **Background:** Used today by "moderates" to prove tolerance. In reality, this verse was revealed when Muhammad was weak.
*   **The Switch: Medinan Context (Power):** 
    *   **Literal Quote (Quran 9:5 - The Sword Verse):** *"And when the sacred months have passed, then kill the polytheists wherever you find them..."*
    *   **Background:** Revealed when Muhammad was strong. According to the doctrine of Abrogation, this verse nullifies the tolerance of 109:6.
*   **The "Ignorance" Trap:** Even if a modern Muslim is ignorant of this history and sincerely believes the Meccan verses are "true Islam," they are still carrying a "timed-release" deception. The moment they or their children become "devout" and study the full text, the Medinan commands (the "real" Islam) are waiting for them. **Ignorance today is merely the precursor to Taqiyya tomorrow.**
