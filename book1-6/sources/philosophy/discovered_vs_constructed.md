# Discovered vs constructed — analysis

**Use:** Part 2 A. Spine: the world is consequential and has natural constraints.

---

## 1. Distinction

| | Constructed (form) | Discovered (substance / constraint) |
|--|-------------------|-------------------------------------|
| **What** | Particular label, ceremony, legal packaging, technical standard | Problem or solution pattern that reappears under human limits |
| **Test** | Could be otherwise without canceling the problem | Reappears when banned; independent cultures reinvent |
| **Policy** | Legitimate redesign space | Decrees bounce; underground return; thrival cost if denied |

---

## 2. Worked cases

### Money
- **Form:** shells, gold coins, paper, bank ledger, digital entries.  
- **Substance:** medium of exchange under specialization and scarcity; solves double coincidence of wants.  
- **Evidence pattern:** When official money is destroyed or hyperinflated, informal media and barter hybrids return.  
- **Assyria / ancient credit:** secondary literature and tablet corpora document credit and exchange long before modern ideology — use if expanding; not required for the logic.

### Language
- **Form:** which phoneme string means “water.”  
- **Substance:** need for stable shared labels for coordination.  
- **Failure mode:** forced weekly redefinition of load-bearing words for political ends → coordination and truth-seeking collapse.

### Family / childrearing
- **Form:** nuclear vs extended household, wedding rites, naming customs.  
- **Substance:** sexual reproduction; long human childhood dependency; need for durable investment by specific adults.  
- **Failure mode:** ideology that children raise themselves or that the state is a full substitute parent without formation cost.

### Rule against murder / basic force
- **Form:** statute wording, court architecture.  
- **Substance:** cooperation collapses if killing is unchecked inside the peace group.  
- Nearly universal prohibition inside the in-group with variation on war/out-group — still a constraint on thrival.

### Roads / traffic
- **Form:** drive left vs right.  
- **Substance:** shared predictable rule to avoid collision.

---

## 3. Logical sequence

1. Limited humans specialize and live past tomorrow.  
2. That creates recurrent problems (exchange, trust, formation, defense).  
3. Solutions have disposable skins (forms) and load-bearing joints (constraints).  
4. Reform the skin when thrival requires.  
5. Denying the joint produces black markets, orphans of the ideology, or empty tables.  
6. “Social construct” talk that erases (5) is a rhetorical weapon, not a discovery.

---

## 4. Errors on both sides

- **Reactionary freeze:** every form is sacred. Fails when form blocks thrival.  
- **Constructivist totalism:** every substance is costume. Fails when reality returns the bill.
