# Plan review commentary — book1-6

**Scope:** `plan/outline_p1.md`, `plan/outline_p2.md`, `plan/dictionary_p1.md`, `plan/dictionary_p2.md` only. Referenced files not reviewed: `absences_vs_prior_books.md`, `sources/`.
**Date:** 2026-07-17
**Reviewer:** external critique (Kimi), after a full read of book1-5 chapters and an extended adversarial discussion of its arguments.

## Verdict

The plan absorbs the strongest objections to book1-5 without losing the spine. The foundation is now honest (affirmed axiom, not fake derivation), the method section M is a real philosophical upgrade, and A3 step 6 is the load-bearing self-critique device — if the writer honors it. Remaining work is discipline in the hard places: name the hardest counterexample, rename B8, hold G to M's standards, keep the dossiers short.

## What improved (mapped to prior objections)

- **B3 axiom layers.** Axiom / corollaries / participation, with the chain labeled "motivations, not fake pure proof," and the dead-earth argument as motivation. "Not a draft into every profession" absorbs the doctor/soldier rebuttal. The foundation can no longer be sunk by attacking a derivation, because no derivation is claimed.
- **De-branding with teeth.** Dropping frame/mirror/bundle/inversion (A1, I4) is made substantive by A3 step 6: *after every failure mode, refute the crude reaction, restate the independent positive.* This institutionalizes symmetric self-critique; held to, the book structurally cannot become the enemy catalog A2 disclaims.
- **Section M resolves the correspondence/pragmatism tension.** "Grade practice and theory apart when they split" (M2); usefulness as clue and filter, not certificate of ontology; wrong vs incomplete with the Ptolemaic/Kepler/Newton chain. "Do not sneer at the older calculators" (M4) is the right tone guard. Placement after axioms, before the virtues bulk, is correct.
- **Theology qualifiers.** H8: occasionalism-style packages named only *when they block reality's veto* — a functional criterion instead of a metaphysical verdict. "Compatible strands exist (secondary-cause realists, falsafa, etc.)" names what book1-5 omitted. B4 hedges "not nation as the only real unit by metaphysics." E9 defines enemies by acts and programs with an opponent contrast class. B5's note ("do not let this eat the positive book") shows the boundary-clause risk is seen from inside.
- **F0 is a genuine addition.** Opening the harm section with the good cushion kills the "let them starve" reading in advance and gives the slide analysis a baseline. F6's design tests (shock or career? bridge or substitute life?) are usable criteria.
- **B8 fairness parenthetical.** Design procedure vs lottery metaphysics is the right distinction (see issue 2 for what remains).

## Open issues (action items)

1. **Name the hardest counterexample [F, H10].** F + H10 describe how to grade premises (history, scale, generations), but the strongest counterexample to the slide thesis — high-trust universalist welfare states persisting for decades — is never named. Without it, decline prediction keeps an unfalsifiable tail: whatever hasn't decayed hasn't decayed *yet*. Fix: name the hardest case in the manuscript and state what evidence would count *against* the slide. This is also the best defense against the hermetic-argument charge (P2.B1) the plan levels at others.

2. **Rename the B8 thread [B8, D15, E12].** The entry now targets only "popular political use" of the veil while keeping "anti-Rawls" in its title. Either engage the actual Rawls text or drop the name. "Marble-self" and "modular identity" carry the whole argument; the Rawls label invites the one specialist review that notes the text was never engaged.

3. **Hold G to M's standards [G, D6].** The two-generation slide into ugliness is a historical-sociological assertion with no stated test protocol, sitting next to the book's most testable method section. "Vitality" is admittedly the least specifiable element of D6. Fix: give the section an evidential discipline (what would count against the slide?) or a marked drop in certainty. Otherwise it reads as taste dressed as axiom.

4. **Watch the cumulative weight of the ending arc [H8, H2–H3, P2.F3].** Ordering rules (packages → test rule → long-horizon → alliances → positive close) are well-chosen, and "do not open with alliance theory" (H1) is right. But H8 + P2.F3 dossiers accumulate. H3's "Israel-as-success under fire" as a named blocker will date the book and polarize readers the method sections could win; the mechanisms checklist works without that example.

5. **Close the tier-justification seam [B4, B8].** B4 is instrumental ("practical reach under limits"); B8 is constitutive (the chain "grounds duties and gratitude"). A critic will ask: when reach changes — logistics shrink distance — do the tiers move? One sentence in B4 stating which justification governs closes it.

6. **Reconsider two demotions.**
   - The five-questions sorting device (book1-5's strongest hook) survives only as H5's "incompatible pairs." Consider restoring it in the opening as the diagnostic on-ramp to the axioms.
   - The matrix grows to seven filters (P2.G1). The "six words" mnemonic discipline was the point; the optional seventh helps, but watch the tool's weight.
