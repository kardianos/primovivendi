## A2 — Judgment, room, and world (world-as-judge)

**Locked:** Neither bare “keep the world” nor a lab-only caveat alone. The house relation is: **man judges under humility; the world does not sit in the room; the world answers claims about what is and what follows; acceptance is not correspondence.**

**House formula (canonical):**

> Each man must judge, for no one else can do his seeing for him; and each man must judge under humility, for he is limited and often wrong. The world does not take its seat in the room where speeches are scored. When a claim is offered as knowledge of what is, or of what will follow from what is, the world answers by what in fact comes to pass. What the company accepts may still prevail among speakers for a time; it does not alter what comes to pass. Truth is the name of a model of reality, rather than of falsehood or of hope.

**Writer rules:**

- Callbacks to “world wins / keep the world / things win” must keep this **sense** (judgment + humility; room ≠ world; answer in what comes to pass; truth ≠ acceptance). Exact wording may vary; sense may not.
- Does not wipe mystery, divine, or non-testable goods: the world’s answer is for claims offered as knowledge of what is or what follows — not a machine that settles every dispute.
- The good (life, thrival, duty) is affirmed elsewhere; consequence does not invent the good, but neither does the room cancel consequence.
- M1 still sections off what the method does not settle.

**Downstream:** C1 scope language across chapters; align bare maxims to this sense.

## A3 — Positive-first (vision, not contrast)

**Locked:** Positive-first. The work of this book is a **positive vision**, not a contrasting survey of schools.

**House sense:**

- State our answers first and primarily.
- Bounds in Part 1 may contain the vision (“it is this, not that”) as a **guiding light** — a short refuse that orients the reader — not as exposition of alternatives.
- Full packages, steelmans, and rival tours belong later (H8 / Part 2 dossiers), not beside every bold axiom head.
- Required refuses (e.g. blank slate; justice not equal end-states / not return in kind) stay as plain boundary sentences after the positive claim. They do not open the paragraph and do not expand into alternative systems.

**Writer rules:**

- Opener: our answers; other answers exist and appear later — do not pledge “beside chief antagonists” or “not the sole conclusion” as the chapter’s stance.
- Body: positive claim → brief “not that” only where the question demands a boundary → no rival parade.
- “Not that” names the boundary; it does not teach the rejected view.

**Downstream:** B1.3 opener; B1.1–B1.2 refuse-after-positive; charge mirrors the same pattern.

## A4 — “Honest scope” (remove; flag, do not blind-replace)

**Locked:** Option (i) sense — the frozen phrase **honest scope / honest scopes** does not stay. It is empty house slop from a prior agent, not load-bearing diction.

**House sense:**

- To know what someone is saying, the speaker must use words that carry meaning. **Honest** adds nothing to **scope** in this frozen pair; the pair as a whole fails that test.
- The phrase must be **removed throughout** manuscript (and plan/whitelist seams that teach writers to reintroduce it).
- **Do not blacklist** the words *honest* or *scope* alone. Either word may appear when it earns its place. What is forbidden is the empty compound and the habit of using it as if it named a doctrine.
- **Do not mechanically replace** every hit with one canned string. Mechanically **flag** (grep / `%%` / pass list); then revise each hit so the sentence states the real claim in words that mean it.

**Preferred plain senses when revising (not mandatory paste strings):**

1. Same rules / same procedure for every person in the same position.
2. Inside a defined membership, role, or jurisdiction (when the bounded set itself must be named).
3. *Scope* alone only when defined in place and doing work.

**Writer / agent rules:**

- Pass C3 (or equivalent): `rg 'honest scope'` → every publish hit rewritten for meaning; exit when the phrase is gone from `chapters/` publish text (notes may record `%% done` then strip).
- Update `vocab_whitelist.txt` if procedural symmetry still defines itself by “honest scope.”
- E5/W7 spirit: one word per concept; no frozen cant that carries no extra meaning.

**Downstream:** C3 as flag-then-revise (not blind substitute); whitelist/plan clean so the phrase is not re-taught.

## A6 — Human particularity / “twins” (assert premise, not studies)

**Locked:** Reserve empirical fashion. The book **offers a standard for examination**; it does not perform the examination of every fact of the age. Claim reality only where the axioms require it. **Do not lean on twin studies or other contemporary study language** in the axioms block.

**House sense — nature of man (working fourfold):**

1. **Limited** — necessary.
2. **Self-interested** (descriptive: survival and near kin) — necessary.
3. **Varied in ability** — increasingly required by the rest of the work; will draw anger, contempt, or willful misunderstanding; still assert as premise, not as lab report.
4. **Product of lineage** — same: particular, not a generic token; inheritance of the body and the line is meaningful; assert without study citation.

The first pair many will accept with minor contention. The last pair is harder and more contested; author judges them increasingly necessary. For this lock: **all four may stand as axioms of the creature**, stated as method/premise, not as results of named studies of this age.

**Writer rules:**

- Soften or cut “studies of twins reared apart… resembling stations.”
- Keep particularity: no generic person; bound to family, lineage, situation; people differ; ability is not uniform; the body and the line matter.
- Close the verify tag by **removing unsourced study claims**, not by inventing sources.
- Positive-first (A3): state what man is; short refuse of blank slate / perfectibility as guiding light where required — not a genetics dossier.
- Matches A5 charge spirit: standard for the reader’s examination, not a substitute for it.

**Downstream:** B1.6, B3 empirical hygiene; anthropology paragraph and charge in `1100`.

## A8 — Style intensity (meaning first; Campbell as clarity, not costume)

**Locked:** Option (i) — **staged.** Content phases (B–C) write clear formal prose, claim-true, without ornament hunt. Phase D applies full W12 / `style/campbell_agent_rules.md` chapter by chapter. Flow (E) last.

**What “Campbell” means for this book (author):**

- The **chief** demand is not period length or 18th-century costume. It is that **every word carry meaning**. If a word can be thrown away without loss, it should not be there (W4 / E3; Campbell on vivacity: fewer words with purity and perspicuity).
- **Metaphors / pictures:** when used, they must be real work — order, logic, vitality (W2) — poetry that earns its place, not lines that could vanish unmissed. Decorative or stacked figures fail A8 and W2.
- Careful emulation of Campbell’s **habits** (define, divide, discriminate; one principal end; perspicuity first) **increases clarity**, which serves the work. That is the point.
- **Not the goal:** “sound like Campbell” as pastiche. **The goal:** each word chosen carefully; each concept dictated clearly.

**Writer rules by phase:**

| Phase | Intensity |
|-------|-----------|
| **B–C (content)** | Clear, formal, complete sentences. House formulas’ sense intact. No forced archaism. Every word still load-bearing — plain is not license for slop. |
| **D (style)** | Full W12 read of Campbell rules + examples; balanced periods where they aid clarity; protect refuses and FIX_PLAN §2 blocks; never delete meaning for euphony. |
| **E (flow)** | Joints and arcs only; no new doctrine; no emptying words for smooth transitions. |

**Downstream:** All agent packets; D8 acceptance = clarity and meaningful diction, not costume score.
