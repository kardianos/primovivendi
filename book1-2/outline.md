  - "society needs common ground", "minimal overlapping consensus" for people of radically different metaphysics (Christians, atheists, agnostics, etc.), while deliberately excluding those who reject reality/truth (post-modernists, critical constructivists).
  - Precepts seek to describe what is good, not why. Many people have different reasons for believing why something is good. -- not a total worldview replacement.

  - Humans are limited, selfish, and not naturally good. -- "Life has limits to what can be done... Life can be good despite the restrictions of reality."
  - Tiered Self-Anchored Responsibility.
    - "At the core of societal principles is the will to live. I desire to live and live well... I divide my will between myself and my children... extended family... community... nation..."

  - Procedural Symmetry
